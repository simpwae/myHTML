FAQ's

Results 
Acceptance of expired CNIC
Situation:
- Acceptance of expired CNIC 
- Acceptance of expired CNIC on the basis of copy of NADRA Token 
- Acceptance of expired CNIC on the basis of copy of valid Passport
Attestation may be allowed on expired CNIC/passport within one month of date of expiry.
Passport (alternate of non-legible copy of CNIC)
Situation:
- If some information on copy of CNIC is not legible, relaxation on the basis of copy of Valid passport
Recommendations: Allowed subjected to matching of particulars with NADRA database.
Transfer of Credit Hours 
Situation:
- 50% Transfer
- More than 50% Transfer 
- Transfer from recognized University/DAI 
- Transfer from Non-recognized University/DAI or Non recognized Campus of recognized University
Recommendations: Transfer of below than 50% courses from a recognized University/DAI are allowed.
Transcript issued by affiliated institute/colleges of HEC recognized Universities 
Situation:
- Transcript issued by the institute or college constituted with the University(chartered with HEC) 
- Transcript issued by the institute or college constituted with the University (chartered with HEC) countersigned by the controller of examination of the University.
- Only degree can be attested on the basis of Transcript issued by the institute or college constituted with the University by checking name, father name, Reg No. Roll No. Marks, CGPA(which will be available)
In case transcript/Marks sheet is not issued by Controller-s/Examination-s office of the university/DAI rather it has been issued by any affiliated college/institute/department, then such transcript/Marks must be counter signed by Controller of Examination of the awarding University/DAIs if its attestation is required from HEC.
Only degree can be attested on the basis of Transcript issued by the institute or college constituted with the University by checking name, father name, Reg No. Roll No. Marks, CGPA(which will be available)
Nomenclature on degree and its transcripts
Situation:
- BE(Computer Engineering) and Bachelor of Computer Engineering
- BSc and BSc (Hons). 
- BSc in Biotechnology & Biochemistry 
- BSc (CS) and BCS
Nomenclature of degree program (title of degree program such Bachelor of Arts, Master of Arts, MPhil in Economic, etc) must be same on degree and transcript.
Requirement of PEC registration
Situation:
- No need of PEC registration Certificate/Card for attestation of Engineering degrees 
- PEC registration Certificate/Card is mandatory for attestation of Engineering degrees 
- Expired PEC registration Certificate/Card is mandatory for attestation of Engineering degrees
- PEC registration Certificate/Card will only be demanded from private sector Universities/DAls.
- Case can be process by only confirmation of Batch from PEC website
Recommendations:
- Requirement of PEC registration Certificate/Card is relaxed till August 31, 2016 and afterword will be demanded as mandatory documents (valid or expired).
- If Batch is accredited and available on PEC website, concerned degree will be attested
What are the documents that are required for attestation when mode of attestation is through Authorized Person?Situation:
- Authority letter only signed by degree Holder
- Authority letter signed by Degree holder as well as attested by Grade-17 officer 
- Scanned Copy of Authority letter attested by Grade-17 officer in original
- More than one attestation case from authorized person will not be allowed at a time
In case application is being submitted by Authorized Person, the following documents are required:
a) Authority letter duly attested by a Grade 17 or above officer for the authorized person.
B) Copy of valid CNIC of authorized person.
Note: Currently Walk-in mode of attestation is suspended
SSC/HSSC Mark sheet as an alternate of SSC,HSSC certificates Situation:
- `O`& `A` levels completed with the deficiency of following subjects: 
1. Islamic Studies 
2. Pak Studies 
3. Urdu 
- After completing Bachelor`s or onward degree applicant passed above mentioned subjects and obtained equivalence from IBCC 
For instance: 
IBCC Equivalence certificates show that applicant completed O & A Levels from 2005-14 (year 2005-2008 and 2014 (only Islamic studies/ Pak Studies/ Urdu) whereas Bachelor has duration(2008-2012)
Applicant is no longer to provide details about previous qualification including SSC and HSSC.
What is the procedure of Correction of name/ Father name on Mark sheet?
Please contact with relevant University for correction of name / Father`s Name.
What is the procedure of change or delete of last name on attestation form?
Applicant can modify/update information by signing-in the HEC attestation profile. If degree already attested then applicant need to apply for post attestation correction by generating a ticket at onlinehelp.hec.gov.pk
What is the procedure of changing password, if forgotten?
Please click on forget password, a code will be send on your given email address. 
Applicant may also call at 111-119-432 or 0334-1119432 for the purpose.
Can we pay -verification fee- through courier?
If applicant has applied through Courier mode of attestation then applicant is required to hand over the application form, required documents and attestation fee to courier company and get a receipt. Please furnish the case via authorized courier company. At present, TCS is authorized to facilitate applicants for degree attestation who applied from different areas within  the country.
What is the procedure of verification of Degree for foreign citizen?
The applicants living abroad have to send their cases to designated office of Gerry`s FedEx at Islamabad. Applicants are advised to check details at www.hec.gov.pk
